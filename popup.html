Elsagate Detector

Detect and Protect

Definition
Elsagate is a video for children that contains violence, pornography, or disturbing scenes.
How to Use
  • 1. Press the button below
  • 2. Wait a moment, the extension will automatically label videos following your scroll, search, or click
  • 3. If a Enjoy, This Video is Safe appears on the video, then the video is safe
  • 4. But if the video is labeled as Warning, Detected as Elsagate , please be careful!
  • 5. If you want to remove a label or see problems with labeling, simply refresh the page!
